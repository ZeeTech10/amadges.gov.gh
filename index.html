Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To prepare all students to become lifelong learners and responsible citizens who are ready to meet the challenges of the future through capacity building of teachers and collaboration with stakeholders of education.

To create a model district that would stand tall among others through efficient and effective use of available resources to achieve the overall goals of education delivery in the country.

About Us

Asene Manso Akroso Education Directorate was carved out from Birim Central Municipal in August 2019
It started with staff strength of twenty-four (24).
Due to lack of accommodation, the Director had to operate briefly from Birim Central Education Office.
The Directorate was relocated to defunct hotel, Fadakyi Lodge, at Manso from January 2019 to February 2021.
It shares boundary with Birim Central to the south, West Akim to the west, Akyemansa to the north, and Agona East to the east.
