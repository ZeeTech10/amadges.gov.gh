About Us as a District

Asene Manso Akroso Education Directorate was carved out from Birim Central Municipal in August 2019

It started with staff strength of twenty-four (24).
Due to lack of accommodation, the Director had to operate briefly from Birim Central Education Office.  

The Directorate was relocated to defunct hotel, Fadakyi Lodge, at Manso from January 2019 to February 2021.
From 2021 till date, the Directorate is currently located at the District Assembly first floor.

It shares boundary with Birim Central to the south, West Akim to the west, Akyemansa to the north, and Agona East to the east. 

The key strategic priorities in the district are:
Providing an environment that enhances learning and development of the whole person:
By making our schools safe.
and providing infrastructure and learning resources.
. Strengthening programmes and practices that support academic achievement of pupils through:
Organization of regular workshops.
And Professional development of staff.
. Improve access to education through:
Expansion of existing structures.
 Opening of new schools closer to the communities.
Improving learning outcomes through:
Effective supervision
Effective appraisal system.
Motivation of teachers.
